Our Team

Bobby Cortez

Physiotherapist

Bobby Cortez, our esteemed Founding Physiotherapist, brings over 15 years of dedicated experience to our clinic. A graduate of the prestigious University of Southern California with a Doctorate in Physical Therapy, Bobby has a proven track record of excellence in patient care. He is a board-certified clinical specialist in orthopedic physical therapy and has completed advanced training in sports rehabilitation. Recognized for his compassionate approach and innovative treatment plans, Bobby has helped hundreds of patients regain their mobility and improve their quality of life.

Liz Smith

Physiotherapist

Liz Smith, our Senior Physiotherapist, is known for her exceptional skill and compassionate care. Holding a Master’s degree in Physical Therapy from the University of Toronto, Liz has over a decade of experience specializing in neurological and pediatric physiotherapy. She is a certified specialist in neurodevelopmental treatment and has received extensive training in pediatric rehabilitation. Her passion for making a difference in the lives of patients shines through in every session, making her an invaluable member of our team.

Arthur Schwartz

Physiotherapist

Arthur Schwartz, a highly regarded physiotherapist at our clinic, brings a wealth of knowledge and experience to our team. With a Doctorate in Physical Therapy from New York University, Arthur has been practicing for over 12 years, focusing on musculoskeletal and sports injuries. He is a certified manual therapist and has extensive training in advanced orthopedic techniques. Arthur’s analytical approach and attention to detail enable him to diagnose and treat complex conditions effectively. His professional yet friendly demeanor ensures that every patient feels comfortable and confident in their path to recovery.

Sue Chen

Massage Therapist

Sue Chen, our talented Massage Therapist, is known for her exceptional ability to alleviate pain and promote relaxation. She holds a certification in Massage Therapy from the renowned Swedish Institute and has over 8 years of experience in the field. Sue specializes in therapeutic massage techniques, including deep tissue, sports massage, and myofascial release. Sue is committed to providing personalized care and regularly tailors her treatments to meet the unique needs of each client. Her soothing touch and expert knowledge have earned her a loyal clientele who appreciate her dedication to their needs.
